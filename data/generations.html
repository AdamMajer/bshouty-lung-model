You have the option of working with a 5-generation or a 15-generation model. Both models represent the whole pulmonary circulation. Results from simulations with the 5-generation model differ from the 15-generation model only at the first decimal level and are considered clinically insignificant. Iterations with the 15-generation model, obviously, take longer to complete. You can switch between 5 and 15-generations under "Options"

The 5-generation model is the default.
