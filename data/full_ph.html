You have the option of working with a normal pulmonary circulation model ("Standard Mode"), which allows you to modify any parameter within any single vessel/capillary or group of vessels. The "Standard Model" option allows you to simulate any scenario within the pulmonary circulation, whether clinically relevant or not. In addition, the model can be used to simulate any pathological or physiological condition. The provided model includes two predefined scenarios simulating Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension and Pulmonary Venous Occlusive Disease. By selecting "PH Mode", you will have the option of selecting either of these diseases.

Disease models can also be (de-)selected under the Disease menu option in the main window.

Because of the large number of vessels, some vessels may not be apparent on the first screen. To see individual vessels and their characteristics you can zoom in and out using either the "Mouse Wheel in combination with the "Ctrl" button or by using the "+/-" button on the keyboard.

To modify any vessel characteristics, double click on the vessel. A window pops up with the parameters that can be modified.

To run the model, press the "Calculate..." button.
