You have the option of working with a normal pulmonary circulation model ("Standard Model"), which allows you to modify any parameter within any single vessel/capillary or group of vessels. The "Standard Model" option allows you to simulate any scenario within the pulmonary circulation, whether clinically relevant or not. In addition, the model can be used to simulate any pathological or physiological condition. The provided model includes two predefined scenarios simulating Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension and Pulmonary Venous Occlusive Disease. By selecting "PH Model", you will have the option of selecting either of these diseases.

Disease models can also be (de-)selected under the Disease menu option in the main window.
