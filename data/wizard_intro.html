This program simulates the behavior of the human pulmonary circulation. The model is a multibranch model that bifurcates sequentially starting from the pulmonary artery (PA) up to the capillaries and on the venous side, the vessels converge and reunite to end in the left atrium (LA). For further details and references Press F1 in the "Main" window after hitting "Cancel".

You have the option of using the help of this "Wizard" or directly choosing a specific module by selecting the appropriate options under the "Options" menu.

Select either "Next" to continue working with the "Wizard" or "Cancel" and go to "Options" to select a specific module.

You can reactivate the "Wizard" by selecting New Model from the Data menu.
